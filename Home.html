Welcome to Edinburgh

The capital of Scotland captivates visitors
with its plentiful history, culture,
and nature. It's located next to
the coastline of the North
Sea and the flowing hills of the Scottish
lowlands. It is filled with cobbled
streets and dotted with ancient archways
but still manages to remain modern
and lively by integrating its
new and old architecture. It is
lined with charming markets,
pubs, and ghost stories so
you are sure to find something
to your fancy in the city of Edinburgh.
